Construyendo.la is a service that connects ONGs and organizations with people. Let's say you help children serving meals for them every day, but you need a new freezer and you can't afford to buy it now. Person X is moving and has a used freezer in pefect conditions. X advertise the product through our service. We send you a notification that someone wants to donate their freezer. If the product meets your needs, we connect you to X. Deal is done.

The users can get involved with the organizations as volunteers or donors through our service. In our first phase, Construyendo.la will be available for the 3 main cities of the country. For the first half of 2015, we expect to cover the whole country, applying filters by region/city.
